Relish

It seems like every day our smartphones create a magical experience making us all go ‘ohh’ and ‘ahh’ at many new apps. However, the challenge with most mobile applications is to keep the ‘ohh’ and ‘ahh’ experience once they start using it.

Like all of these applications, Relish, an application that integrates the use of video and music together, had this challenge. The idea and the functionality was there, but the ‘ohh’s and ‘ahh’s didn’t stick.

As the UX/UI Designer brought in my SozenTech, I was tasked to redesign the application. To make it easy to use, as well as bring that excitement and seamless experience between music and video.
