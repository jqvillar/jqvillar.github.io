Hello again! I'm Janine -"J-9" to some- and I’ve been exploring, discovering, and creating digital products since 2016. I’ve also had the pleasure of working at SozenTech as a Jr. UX/UI Designer from Oct 2017 to June 2018.


These days you'll find me learning about logo creation and branding as well as expanding my UX/UI knowledge by doing some mini projects. When I'm not doing that, you'll most likely find me playing sports or at various boba shops in Calgary attempting to drink the city's supply of boba. I love sharing what I've learned with others so I instruct and facilitate a condensed Design Sprint workshop for SozenTech from time to time.


I started out with UX/UI design when I fell in love with digital products. I love how a single app can make such a huge difference in someone's life. The thought of being able to create good products that can help people really inspires me and pushes me to learn and explore more of the digital world and the products that live in it.
