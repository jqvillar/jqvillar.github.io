Exploring,
Learning,
& Creating

Hello and welcome! My name is Janine and I’m a UX/UI Designer currently based in Calgary, Canada. I’ve been exploring, discovering, and creating digital products since 2016. I’ve also had the pleasure of working at SozenTech as a Jr. UX/UI Designer from Oct 2017 to June 2018. These days I’ve been learning more about logo creation and branding on top of expanding my UX/UI knowledge by doing some personal projects. Occasionally, I also instruct and facilitate a condensed Sprint workshop for SozenTech.

I started out with UX/UI design because I fell in love. I fell in love with digital products when I realized that I use a lot of them to help better my life. The thought of being able to create good products that can help people really inspired me (and still inspires me) and pushed me to start exploring the world of digital products.

My exploration first lead me to the “daily UI challenge” where I found out how challenging making a sign up page was. Day one made my brain numb after a lot of , “Where would the buttons go?”, “Where would this input field go?”, “WHAT ARE COLORS?!”. I was surprised at how much I didn’t know. To be honest, I still feel that there’s a lot that I still I don’t know and to me that’s okay. If I knew everything and have made everything that means there’s nothing for me to do and i’d miss out on a lot of cool stuff. That’s why it’s difficult for me to say where I am exactly in my UX/UI design journey but I can definitely say I’m enjoying it a lot. I get to meet, get to know, talk to, teach and create with lots of cool people. I also get involved in the process of making cool stuff that people can enjoy. Who doesn’t love that?
