Hello again! I'm Janine a UX/UI Designer currently based in Calgary, Canada. I’ve been exploring, discovering, and creating digital products since 2016. I’ve also had the pleasure of working at SozenTech as a Jr. UX/UI Designer from Oct 2017 to June 2018.

These days I’ve been learning more about logo creation and branding on top of expanding my UX/UI knowledge by doing some personal projects. Occasionally, I also instruct and facilitate a condensed Sprint workshop for SozenTech.

I started out with UX/UI design when I fell in love with digital products. I love how a single app can make such a huge difference in someone's life. The thought of being able to create good products that can help people really inspires me and pushes me to learn and explore more of the digital world and the products that live in it.

My exploration first lead me to the “daily UI challenge” where I found out how challenging making a sign up page was. Day one made my brain numb after a lot of , “Where would the buttons go?”, “Where would this input field go?”, "What is it?", I was surprised at how much I didn’t know.

There’s a lot that I still don’t know and that’s okay. If I knew and made everything, that means there’s nothing for me to do and I’d miss out on a lot of cool stuff. Cool stuff like getting to meet, to know, talk to, teach and create with lots of cool people. I also get involved in the process of making cool stuff that people can enjoy. Who'd wanna miss out on that?
